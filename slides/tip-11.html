Tip 11: Learn to fish

Who wants to be at mercy of someone else?

You have to master every piece of infrastructure your code or process rides on top of. Knowing how all the important stuff works enables you to develop solid code for a platform and to troubleshoot when something goes wrong.

"Ask for a fish, eat for a day. Ask someone to teach you to fish, eat for a lifetime”. Better yet, don’t ask, go teach yourself.
