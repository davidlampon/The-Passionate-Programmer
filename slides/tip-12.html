Tip 12: Learn how business really work

Before making an investment in learning the ins and outs of a business domain you should make sure you are investing in the right one for you and for the state of the market.

But one body of knowledge won’t be outdated soon: basics of business finance. Business is itself a domain of knowledge that we must learn.

How are you supposed to creatively help the business be profitable if you don’t understand how the business work?
