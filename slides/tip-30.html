Tip 30: Say “No”

The quickest path to missing your commitments is to make commitments that you know you can’t meet.

We are put in a spot and we don’t want to disappoint our leader or colleagues so we agree on impossible work to be done in impossible timelines. Saying “yes” is an addictive and destructive habit.

Repeatedly missing commitments destroy any chance of trust. If you are unsure “I don’t know” is a correct answer almost always.
