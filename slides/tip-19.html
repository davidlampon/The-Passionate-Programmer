Tip 19: Right now

Parkinson’s law states that “work expands so as to fill the time available for its completion”. This is an empirical observation not a universal law.

A sense of urgency is enough to double or triple your productivity as decisions are done at the moment and boring tasks can’t be postponed.

It’s better getting it done instead of talking about getting it done. Treat your projects as a race instead of a prison cell.
