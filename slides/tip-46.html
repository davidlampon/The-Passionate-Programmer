Tip 46: Path with no destination

You typically spend all your time doing things and little of your time reaching goals. It’s the same with your career what is important is not the promotions or the salary raises but the time you spend working towards or regardless those advances.

It has no logical end, the path is the goal. Focussing on the end makes you forget to make the process good and bad processes make bad products.

It’s how you traverse the path what is important not the destination.
