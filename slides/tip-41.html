Tip 41: Release your code

Imagine how much easier it would be to find a job if there were companies already relying on software you had written. That’s what you want.

Luckily, we have open source and it’s main stream. Doing this altruistic apportions is a way to climb in the social chain of community. They are building a name for themselves and building a reputation in the industry.

Apart form that contributing to open source shows your passion and commitment.
