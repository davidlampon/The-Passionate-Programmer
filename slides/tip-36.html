Tip 36: Being present

You have the advantage of being face to face with your leaders and your business customers. Don’t squander the opportunity.

Physical proximity is an advantage in the workplace. Not only we see greater productivity and enhanced communication from face-to-face interactions but we also form tighter personal bonds.

Try to actually learn about the people you work with. Not only does it more firmly entrench you into your company but it’s a more enriching way to live.
