Tip 44: Already obsolete

Today’s hot new item is tomorrow’s obsolete junk with a limited shelf life. Your shiny new skills are already obsolete. If you are not into learning the big next thing it’s almost too late.

Start thinking ahead with your study. It’s a bit of a gamble but it’s a game you’ll definitely will loose if you don’t play.

Worst case is that you learned something enriching that isn’t directly applicable to your job. Best case is that you remain ahead of the curve and can continue being an expert in leading-edge technologies.
