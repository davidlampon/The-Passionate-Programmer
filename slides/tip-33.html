Tip 33: Perceptions, perschmeptions

Don’t pretend you don’t care about what other people think about you. You should care because perception is reality.

There is no way to objectively measure the quality of a knowledge worker nor the quality of their work. You are going to be judge by someone’s else perception of you.

If you know what drives other people perception about you the more likely is that you can make them your happy costumers.
